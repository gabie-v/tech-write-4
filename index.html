About Us


Celestial Jewels is founded by a group who has always been interested in jewelry, especially their colleague Shantelle. Celestial Jewels sells traditional Chinese jewelry, that is not only exclusive to China but also to the whole world. We present to you products that is made with pure love and care.


This company made this business for the intention of showing the true beauty and culture of China that is evident in their accessories. Ceslestial Jewels decided to showcase their culture despite the criticism they are facing.
